System Architecture

7 Modules. One Platform.

Every piece of the donation pipeline — from contributor registration to AI-powered freshness checks — built into a single cohesive system.

01

Registration & Authentication

Separate registration flows for Contributors and NPO Centres. Secure authentication management for all user types.

Contributor Signup NPO Onboarding Auth Management
02

Orders Management (NPO Portal)

View daily orders with full drill-down to contribution line items. Approve or reject orders, and print sticker labels for approved boxes.

Daily Orders Approve / Reject Print Stickers
03

Reports (NPO Portal)

Daily contribution summaries, historical orders by date range or contributor, and weekly champion leaderboards.

Daily Summary History Reports Weekly Champions
04

Orders Screen (Contributor Portal)

Contributors view past orders, create new contributions with target date and line items (description, quantity, time made), and generate QR codes for centre drop-off.

New Order Line Items QR Code Print
05

Google Maps Service

Automatically generate an optimised daily route for food pickup and delivery, output as a ready-to-use Google Maps URL.

Route Generation Maps URL Daily Logistics
06

AI Freshness Service

AI-powered check that analyses TimeMade and item description to determine whether food donations are still fresh and safe to distribute.

Freshness Check AI Analysis Food Safety
07

Push Email Service

Automated email reminders for heavy contributors who go quiet, personalised guidance for private/non-kosher/Saturday contributors, and approval/rejection notifications.

Contributor Reminders Smart Notifications Order Alerts
